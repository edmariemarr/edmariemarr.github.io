CasaCinema

Overview

CasaCinema is based on a fictional local movie theater. The app presents a way for users to purchase movie tickets, and view the available and upcoming films to watch along with their trailers, information, and even user reviews.

Duration

October 2022 to March 2023

Problem

How can the CasaCinema app present a way for users to view the current movies in rotation as well as those coming out soon? Can we present movie trailers and user reviews to help guide customers to the movie they'd like to see? How can we make purchasing tickets as straight-forward as possible?

Many users want to enjoy a movie at the theater but struggle with choosing which one. They’d also like a simple way to purchase tickets remotely.

Goal

Design an app for CasaCinema that will let users view movie trailers, user reviews, and information before ticket purchase.

Pain Points

  1. Difficulty choosing

    2. Many users need help narrowing down their choices on what movie to watch in theaters.

  2. Too busy

    3. Users need a way to easily get notified of movies, since many students, parents, and workers are too busy to notice when a movie they’d watch is available.

  3. In-person ticket purchase

    4. Users need a way to purchase tickets remotely. That way they don’t need to wait until the day-of to purchase the ticket at the theater.
