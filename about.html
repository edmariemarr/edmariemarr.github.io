Hi! I'm Edmarie and I’m a software engineer based in San Juan, PR.

Leveraging my background in engineering and design, I’m committed to presenting user-driven ideas that are both creative and technically viable. My passion for technology and curiosity about human behavior inspire me to craft designs that not only address real-world problems but also improve the way users interact with digital spaces.
