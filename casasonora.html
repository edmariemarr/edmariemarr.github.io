CasaSonora

Overview

CasaSonora is based on a fictional small music venue. The website presents a way for users to view the upcoming gigs for the venue. Users can also purchase tickets for their desired show.

Duration

February 2023 to March 2023. Updated design November 2024.

Problem

How can this website show users what artists are playing at CasaSonora right as they're announced? What could we do to make sure users can be redirected to find out more about the artists playing? How can they purchase tickets when they're on high demand?

This fictional venue wants to provide a simple way for users to see upcoming shows, research the artists playing, and for them to be able to purchase tickets.

Goal

Design a website for CasaSonora that will let users view upcoming gigs, and let them purchase tickets.

Pain Points

  1. Have all in one place

    2. Users would like to easily view the event schedules by month or even weekly.

  2. Need for notifications

    3. Many would appreciate a way to get notified of upcoming shows and when tickets would be available.

  3. In-person ticket purchase

    4. Users need a way to purchase tickets remotely. That way they don’t need to wait to purchase the ticket at the venue.
